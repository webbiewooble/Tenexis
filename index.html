About Us

Redefining Surgical Standards.

Tenexis Medical Private Limited, located in Hyderabad's premier life sciences hub, is dedicated to the precise engineering of surgical instruments. We empower healthcare professionals with tools that meet the highest global standards.

We blend advanced automated metallurgy with rigorous quality control to produce instruments that offer zero-drag penetration and ultimate tactile feedback. Our focus is singular: uncompromising quality for critical procedures.
